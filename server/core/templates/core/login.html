Welcome to NOC Lab Dementia Assessment Website

This website is built on the result of the research study entitled “Assessment of Dementia from Drawing Tests using AI Methods”, led by Professor Ioannis Paschalidis of the NOC Lab of Boston University. The objective is to collect diverse data, which will enable the development of better and more reliable Artificial Intelligence (AI) models that assess the subject’s cognitive status and determine whether it can be characterized as normal or whether it is consistent with cognitive decline that may be associated with Alzheimer’s disease and other forms of dementia.
